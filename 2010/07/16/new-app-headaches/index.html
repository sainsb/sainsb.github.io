External GIS app user complaints

The most common problems so far:

1)    Inability to set custom scale
I hope to research this more in the next month.  I have not found any scripts/forum stubs regarding this a la the new ESRI APIs.  This was mostly unforeseen.
2)    Inability to mark coordinates on the map
This is developed and tested and will be pushed into prod when I get back.  I would do it before I leave but then it would inevitably fail ;P.
3)    Inability to print/export.
I didn’t realize the breadth of browser pop-up blockers.  When I have walked people through disabling them in IE it usually still fails.  I later discover that it a 3rd party toolbar…I’ve put comprehensive help in the FAQs for this.
4)    Inability to measure in IE7.
One person reported this…I cannot reproduce it…but have taken note.
5)    Blank map when printing from the print_parcel.aspx page with IE7.
Little issues keep coming up for people with IE7 that I cannot reproduce.
6)    X layer doesn’t appear at Y scale…
I am making note of these and can adjust accordingly.
7)    No aerial photo coverage
I tiled the NAIP imagery but have now decided to go with a dynamic layer instead so as to get coverage (albeit crappy coverage) at >1:10,000 scales.
